Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is the goal of IQ Lounge?

The goal of IQ Lounge is to provide students with a means of staying connected and interacting with other students during these hard times of social distancing.

How do I create an account?

In order to create an account, you can click the "Login" button located at the top right of the website and then select "Create New Account".

Do I need to create an account to access the discussion boards?

You do! In order to view and contribute to any of the disucssion boards provided, you must create an account first.

Who is the target audience of IQ Lounge

Students! We solely created IQ Lounge to give students of any level an enviroment that allows them to socialize as if they were on campus.

Are there going to be additional features in the future?

Absolutely! We are currently at a beta stage of IQ Lounge and have many additional ideas and features to implement in the future.

Who created IQ Lounge?

IQ Lounge is developed by a team of five hard working individuals who have come together to complete projects for their college course. Click the "About Us" button at the bottom to find out more.
